How I See It

Pia

studies graphic design
in Sint-Lukas in Gent, Belgium

In my work, I first started, and still continue today, by an observation of the world around me. I want to show different point of vues en try to show to other people my vision. For that, I do links between different pictures, and also try to make associations to create a new image. I use a imagery full of symbols, photo’s and objects of the everyday life. I am on search for details and try to react on a graphic way to show my vision on the world.
