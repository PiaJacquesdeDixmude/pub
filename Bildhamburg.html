BildHamburg
 

mobirise.com

After a school trip in Hamburg where we had to make series of photos, the assignment was to made a publication in a newspaper format. The aim was to give your vision on one or several aspects of Hamburg, and to associate a text with the photos to add a new layer to the publication. This allowed us to learn to create visual information on several pages. The publication had to contain 8 pages. An interesting aspect of this project was the creation of a common Flickr account with all the photos taken by the students during the trip. This permitted to easily share the photos and to have an access to an important source of different images of Hamburg. I had taken pictures of doors and also of city landscapes. My newspaper was like a walk in the city of Hamburg. Each door was like an entrance to an other place. A bit like if in your mind it took you somewhere else. To give this effect I displayed different photos on each other to create a new poetic and melancholic image. To create a contrast with these images, I linked them with German poems of Bach (who lived in Hamburg) in the typical German typography, Fraktur.
