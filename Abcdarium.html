Abcdarium

To learn their alphabet to children, teachers and parents often use alphabet posters of alphabet books. The letters of the alphabet are often linked with a word: A like Apple, B like Boy, etc. The assignment was to make your own abcdarium, but for adults, it had to have 28 pages, with each page dedicated to a letter. My abcdarium called “As Easy As ABC” is based on English expressions.

They are presented with images illustrating the expression litterally. This created a humorous image that played with the fact that expressions have two ways of being interpreted. When you don’t know an expression and you hear it for the first time it gives you always a strange image at first hand. I decided to base my abcdarium on this and show how I saw expression when you don’t know his meaning. I choose to do my book in English because for me English expressions are really a characteristic of this language and are numerous. I see it as a playful book between language and images.
